Welcome to Andromeda Care

We are Andromeda Care. Our mission is to help you and anyone around you become educated in HIV/AIDS issues and spread a better sex education standard.

We are stationed in the Bay Area, and work from the STEM program SMASH. You can learn more about each individual team memeber in our Contact page. You can learn about our history in our Background page. With our Curriculum, you can educate yourself, your students, and anyone around you on sex ed. In our resources page, you will find all sorts of websites which can also provide you with information about HIV/AIDS and sexual education, as well as any sources we used.

Join us in support of those who live with HIV and AIDS, in the fight against its spread and the demand for better sex ed.
