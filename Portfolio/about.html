About Alexa

Life

I was born in Castro Valley, but I have lived in East Oakland my whole life. I live with my mom, dad and younger brother. I have a grey kitten named Kogane. I am mexican-american, and my family comes from a small pueblo; Mixtlan, Jalisco. All of my family lives in Mexico, except my aunt and her small family. I travel to Mexico every year to visit them, and I get to see not only family but friends.
